Welcome to the Logging & Threat Detection Assessment

This assessment is designed to help your organization evaluate and strengthen its security posture by reviewing key controls across the full set of Microsoft Cloud Security Benchmark (MCSB) recommendations. While based on MCSB, the principles covered here are widely applicable to other security frameworks and best practices.

The focus of this assessment is to ensure that your organization is collecting the recommended logs effectively. By completing it, you will gain actionable insights into how to collect, centralize, and analyze logs in your SIEM solution, enabling better detection of threats, faster investigation, and improved overall security operations.
